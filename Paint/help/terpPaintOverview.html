terppaint1 Overview

terppaint1 is a drawing tool you can use to create simple or elaborate drawings. These drawings can be either black-and-white or color and can be saved as a bitmap, jpeg or gif file. You can print your drawing, use it for your desktop background, or paste it in another document. You can also use terppaint1 to view and edit scanned photos. You can also create a slide show with pictures in a folder.
