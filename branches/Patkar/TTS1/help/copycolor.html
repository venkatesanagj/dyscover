To copy color from one area or object to another

  1. In the toolbox, click .
  2. Click the area containing the color you want to copy.
  3. In the toolbox, click .
  4. Click the object or area where you want the new color.

Notes

  • Copying a color using the eyedropper tool will change your foreground color.
  • If you copy a color with the eyedropper tool by right-clicking, you will change your background color.
